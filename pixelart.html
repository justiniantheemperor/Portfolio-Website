Pixel Artwork

In my freetime, I like to make artwork resembling the characters from the Pokémon games on the Game Boy Advance. This artform is trickier than it looks, since it is limited in the number of colors, size of the canvas, and level of detail. Lately, I have been creating my own ficticious versions of Pokémon centered around an aquatic theme for an upcoming project. I do not sell any of my artwork, and I credit every artist I pull royalty-free artwork from. You can view more of my work here.
